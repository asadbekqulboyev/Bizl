What is Bizl and how does it work?
Bizl is a digital business card platform that allows you to create and share a personalized online profile containing all your essential contact information, links, social media profiles, and more. Instead of carrying paper cards, you simply share your unique Bizl link or QR code with anyone you meet. They can then access your complete profile instantly on their phone or computer.
What information can I include on my Bizl profile?
You can include a wide range of information, such as your photo, name, title, company, contact details (phone, email, address), website links, social media profiles (LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, etc.), portfolio links, and even custom links to special offers or promotions. You control exactly what information you want to share.
How do I share my Bizl profile?
Sharing your Bizl profile is easy! You can share it via a unique link, a QR code that can be scanned with any smartphone. You can also share it through email, text message, or social media.
What happens if I update my information on Bizl?
Whenever you make a change to your Bizl profile, it's automatically reflected for everyone who has access to your link or has scanned your QR code.
